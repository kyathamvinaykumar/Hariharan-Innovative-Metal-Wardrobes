About Us

Client Photo / Workshop Image

Since 1990, Hariharan Innovative Metal Wardrobes has established itself as a trusted leader in premium steel almirahs and modular metal furniture. We began with a straightforward vision and have continuously pushed the boundaries of quality and design.

Boasting 100% in-house production, we maintain complete control over our manufacturing process. We source only the finest quality steel materials, combining them with precision engineering and careful craftsmanship to construct exceptionally sturdy frameworks.

Our core values are centered on unmatched durability, seamless customization, and complete customer satisfaction. We ensure every unit not only secures your belongings safely but does so with style tailored explicitly to your own space.

Ready to upgrade your space? We encourage you to contact us for a personalized consultation or to explore our products online to find the exact match for your needs.
